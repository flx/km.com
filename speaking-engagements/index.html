Event / Screening / Speaking Engagements

Kyoko Miyake is available for speaking engagements, screening events and moderating panels. She has been a guest speaker in events internationally, including National Film School (Denmark), Japan Now at the British Library (UK), and many others. Events can include screening her films and facilitating discussions about filmmaking, visual journalism, cross cultural experience, and others. She has served on jury at film festivals including Hot Docs (Canada) and Sheffield DocFest (UK).
